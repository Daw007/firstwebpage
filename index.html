Yellow Claw Fan Page!

Bio

Yellow Claw is a Dutch DJ and record production duo from Amsterdam consisting of Jim (born Jim Taihuttu) based in Bali and Nils (born Nils Rondhuis) based in Amsterdam.
The duo's music is a mix of a wide range of genres and often incorporates elements from trap, hip hop, dubstep, hardstyle and moombahton.
